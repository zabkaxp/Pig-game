Welcome to the Pig Game!
On a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible, adding up the numbers rolled on the die. However, if a player rolls a 6, the player's turn is over and any points they have accumulated during this turn are forfeited. Rolling a 6 doesn't wipe out your entire score, just the total earned during that particular roll. A player can also choose to hold if they do not want to take a chance of rolling a 6. If the player chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to his or her score. When a player reaches a total of 100 or more points( that is the default number), the game ends and that player is the winner.
